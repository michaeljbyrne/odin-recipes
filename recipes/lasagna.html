Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the world's greatest dish, it also happens to be my favorite food. There is no bias in the previous statement. It is layers of pasta with cheese in between, usually covered with a red bolognese sauce. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1/2lb ground beef
  • 1/2lb ground pork
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 36oz diced tomato
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • italian seasoning
  • parsley

Steps

  1. layer pasta with cheese
  2. make sauce
